## 3. Orthodox significance

- **Saint and Martyr** — early Eastern Orthodox bishop-martyr
- **The Banquet of the Ten Virgins** is the **most-read Greek patristic text on Christian virginity** alongside Gregory of Nyssa's *On Virginity*
- His **anti-Origenist resurrection theology** prefigures the later Origenist controversies and the Constantinople II condemnations
